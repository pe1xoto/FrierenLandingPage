Beyond Journey’s End follows Frieren, an elven mage who defeated the Demon King alongside a group of heroes. As her friends grow old and pass away, Frieren lives on — reflecting on the passage of time, the value of relationships, and what it truly means to be alive.

FRIEREN

An elven mage who has lived for centuries. She was a member of the group of heroes that defeated the Demon King, but while her companions grow old and pass away, Frieren lives much longer.

フリーレン

FERN

A young mage and Frieren's pupil. She is mature for her age, responsible, and hard-working — acting as the team's emotional anchor during difficult moments.

フェルン

STARK

A brave warrior who was orphaned as a child. He is timid and unsure of his own abilities but shows great courage in battle alongside Frieren and Fern.

シュタルク

HIMMEL

The hero who defeated the Demon King alongside Frieren. He was a kind, compassionate swordsman who left a deep impact on the people he met.
